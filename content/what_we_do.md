The [London Calling website](https://london-calling.org.uk/) collates the latest opportunities from partners for London's not-for-profits, all in one place. London Calling is an initiative of Resource for London and London Plus, funded by City Bridge Foundation. The aim is to help connect London's thousands of charities to relevant events, training, and funding opportunities taking place across the capital.

Good With Data developed a bespoke approach that scrapes data from partner websites and employs generative AI to classify and enhance this information. Our methodology includes partner consent and support for the project.

  <h3 style="margin: 0;">Islington People's Rights: Debt Modernisation</h3>
</div>

[Islington People's Rights](https://www.ipradvice.org.uk/), founded in 1969, work to assist the people
of Islington with welfare benefits and debt issues. Their work enables many Islington residents to increase their income and to deal with their debts, which also has a positive impact on the local community. IPR operate a telephone support line that deals with ~9000 calls a year, many of which are from
people outside of IPR's remit. 

Good With Data developed an AI powered triage chatbot for IPR's website that either automatically refers users to IPR or signposts them to other services. The triagebot aims to ease the pressure on IPR from  out-of-scope enquiries and provide a point of contact outside of the helpline hours. This was funded as part of the Debt Modernisation project.
